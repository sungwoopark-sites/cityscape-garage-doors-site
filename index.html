About CityScape

28+ Years of Honest, Reliable Service

CityScape Garage Doors is a locally owned, licensed, and fully insured garage door company serving Orange County since the late 1990s. Our mission is providing honest, reliable garage door services that exceed our customers' expectations.

We carry top brands including CHI, Martin, Amarr, LiftMaster, Marantec, and Genie. As an International Door Association (IDA) member and California Operator and Door Association (CODA) member, we are held to the highest industry standards.
